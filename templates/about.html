ABOUT US

Welcome to Foreign Languages Academy Gurukulam (F.L.A.G.), your trusted partner in achieving linguistic excellence. Founded in 2018, F.L.A.G. has become a premier language institute in Kerala, specialising in German language training. As the only authorised German Skill Development Centre in the state, we collaborate with German companies to offer industry-specific training that prepares students for success in academic and professional settings. Our approach is inspired by the traditional Gurukula model, fostering a close-knit learning community where personalised guidance and cultural immersion are central to the experience. Our curriculum, meticulously designed by C1-C2 qualified trainers and approved by German industries, equips students with practical language skills and real-world readiness. With campuses in Kochi and Kottayam, including residential facilities for female students, we provide a fully immersive learning environment. Our teaching methodologies, such as the Native German Speaking (NGS) and Language Exposure for Real Life (LERL) approaches, emphasise interactive and practical learning. From one-on-one speaking sessions to our state-of-the-art studio theatre for immersive language practice, we make learning both engaging and effective. Under the leadership of Sujin C Cherian, F.L.A.G. has empowered thousands of students to build successful careers in Germany. With flexible online training, robust campus placement opportunities, and a growing international student base, F.L.A.G. continues to set new benchmarks in language education. Embark on your journey to global opportunities with us. Together, let us speak the future, today.

OUR VISION

At Foreign Languages Academy Gurukulam (F.L.A.G.), our vision is to be a global leader in language education, empowering individuals to become global citizens with strong multi-linguistic skills that eliminate the geographical border. By fostering linguistic excellence and cultural understanding, we aim to equip learners with the skills needed to embrace global opportunities and thrive in an interconnected world. Through innovative teaching methods, personalised support, and practical skill-building, we are dedicated to shaping a future where language becomes a key to success.
