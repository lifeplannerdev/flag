GERMAN LANGUAGE LEVELS

A1

Learn the basics of the German language with A1 level training. Master introductions, simple conversations, numbers, days, and essential grammar like verbs, articles, and sentence structure. Build vocabulary for daily life, travel, and work. Perfect for beginners to start their German journey with confidence and cultural understanding.

A2

Advance your German skills with A2 level training. Enhance your vocabulary, hold detailed conversations, and understand simple texts. Master past and future tenses, modal verbs, and more complex grammar. Learn to discuss personal interests, daily routines, and practical topics, preparing you for deeper cultural and professional interactions in German-speaking environments.

B1

Achieve independence in German with B1 level training. Engage in detailed conversations, express opinions, and comprehend everyday texts. Master complex grammar, including subjunctive forms and advanced sentence structures. Gain confidence in navigating real-life situations like work, travel, and social interactions, while deepening cultural insights and preparing for higher education or career opportunities in German-speaking countries.

B2

Master intermediate German with B2 level training. Improve fluency in conversations on a variety of topics, including current events and abstract concepts. Perfect your command of complex grammar structures, including passive voice and conditional sentences. Enhance your ability to write essays and engage in discussions, preparing for professional or academic opportunities in German-speaking environments.
